Welcome to Ask Stella
Your trusted resource for interview success and career growth.

I’m Stella, an HR professional with over 15 years of global experience and a former Bar Raiser in Training at Amazon. I’ve supported recruitment across a wide range of industries—including retail, finance, education, manufacturing, and technology—and helped candidates secure roles from entry-level positions to staff engineer roles.

Since launching my interview coaching services in late 2024, I’ve worked with job seekers across North America, providing strategic guidance to help them navigate the hiring process with clarity and confidence.

At Ask Stella, I focus on:

Career direction

Resume polishing

Job offer strategies

Mock interview practice

Confidence building

Practical tools to help you get hired

Each week, I share actionable advice, proven frameworks, and real-world insights from the other side of the hiring table. Whether you’re early in your career or aiming for your next big move, this is where you’ll find clarity, structure, and support.
